來源：TechCrunch — https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/24/openai-unveils-its-first-custom-chip-built-by-broadcom/

## 3. 美國用電需求預估續創新高，AI 資料中心成主因之一

來源：Reuters — https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/us-power-use-beat-record-highs-2026-2027-ai-use-surges-eia-says-2026-07-07/

來源：Reuters — https://www.reuters.com/business/unchecked-ai-progress-may-pose-catastrophic-risks-un-panel-warns-2026-07-01/
